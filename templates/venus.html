About Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun, and is Earth's closest neighbor in the solar system. It is the brightest object in the sky after the Sun and the Moon, and sometimes looks like a bright star in the morning or evening sky. The planet is a little smaller than Earth, and is similar to Earth inside.
