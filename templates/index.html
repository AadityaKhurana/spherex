100% Element Neutrality

Each trip has a measurable impact on universal warming. We evaluate the amount of elemant emissions related to each of our travellers and work out the number of measures that need to taken to negate the effect and prevent Space Debris .We off-set 100% of the element footprint generated by our client and staff flights - and also ground transportation - through our financing of large recycling projects around the world. We aim to fight universal warming by taking a highly practical approach aimed at having a strong local social and economic impact. We believe that we are the first company in the Space travel market to be taking such a step.
